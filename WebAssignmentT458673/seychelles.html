We saw almost too many zebra, giraffe, gnus, elephants, tsessebees, waterbuck, impala, red lechwe, kudu, baboons, reed buck, mongoose, various eagles, warthogs, jackals, buffalo, sable antelope. Best Time to Visit the Okavango Delta

Season Months Highlights
Dry Season (Peak Flooding) June - October Best for wildlife viewing, mokoro safaris, fewer mosquitoes, warm days, cool nights.
Green Season (Rainy) November - April Lush scenery, great for birdwatching, newborn animals, lower water levels.
Shoulder Season May & Early November Transition period, rising water levels (May), good wildlife before rain starts (Nov).
